# Touchstones

> Whole-song reference works by other artists. These are songs you return to as
> directional standards — not for a single line (that goes in the inspiration library),
> but for the way the entire song holds a feeling, makes a structural move, or nails a
> concept across all its parts. Touchstones calibrate macro-craft.

## How to add an entry

Copy the entry block below, paste it under `## Entries`, and fill it in. The point is
to capture *what the whole song does* — the feeling it holds, the move it makes, the
shape of its arc. If you find yourself wanting to quote a specific line, that line
probably belongs in `inspiration-library.md` instead. Cross-link the two with the
`Linked library entries` field when both apply.

```markdown
### Song Title — Artist (Year, Album)
- **What it captures:** the feeling/topic/concept the whole song nails — say it precisely
- **What it does well:** the writing/structural/musical moves worth drawing from
- **Why it's a touchstone:** the personal pull — what makes you keep coming back
- **Linked library entries:** [excerpt title], [excerpt title] (optional — quoted lines
  from this song that also live in inspiration-library.md)
- **Tags:** themes, techniques, moods — free-form
- **Added:** YYYY-MM-DD
```
