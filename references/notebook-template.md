# Notebook

> Songwriter's notebook — your own seeds, scenes, titles, characters, palettes, and
> structural experiments. Things you want to write but haven't yet. The skill reads this
> at session start and can surface relevant seeds when you ask "what should I work on?"
> or describe a feeling that maps to one. When a seed becomes a song, mark it spawned
> and link it.

## How to add an entry

Copy the entry block below, paste it under `## Entries`, and fill it in. Be specific —
"a song about loss" doesn't help; "a song from the cat's POV watching us argue" does.
The `Why it's pulling` field is optional but useful when you come back to a stale seed
and want to remember why it mattered.

```markdown
### [Short title or the phrase itself]
- **Type:** scene | title | character | palette | structure | observation | other
- **Seed:** prose description of the idea — what it is, what's pulling at you
- **Why it's pulling:** the emotional or technical thing that won't let go (optional)
- **Status:** open | in-progress | spawned | parked
- **Spawned:** [Song Title] (only if status=spawned, links into Songs Created)
- **Linked touchstone:** [Touchstone entry title] (optional — if a touchstone is steering this)
- **Tags:** themes, techniques, moods — free-form
- **Added:** YYYY-MM-DD
```
